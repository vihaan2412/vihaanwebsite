Countries Significant for Rare Earths

The Largest Producers

The largest rare earth mineral-producing country in the world is the Republic of China because of its location and development in technology. After the Republic of China, the largest producers are the United States, Myanmar, Australia, Thailand, Madagascar, India, Russia, Brazil, Vietnam, and Burundi.

The Largest Consumers

The largest rare earth mineral-consuming country in the world is, again the Republic of China. After the Republic of China, the largest consumers are Japan, the United States of America, and the European Countries.
